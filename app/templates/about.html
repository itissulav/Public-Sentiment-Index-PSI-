Understanding the Pulse of the Nation

The Public Sentiment Index (PSI) systematically measures and analyzes the collective emotions, opinions, and attitudes of the public on major national issues using state-of-the-art Transformer models.

Discover How

The Problem Scenario

Currently, sentiment measurement remains largely informal and fragmented. A huge gap exists between public expectations and deliverables by economic, political, and social institutions.

  • Lack of trusted programs to measure the public mood.
  • Inconsistent data about citizens' reactions to topics.
  • Limited access to real-time information.
  • Social media algorithms shielding users from opposing views.

The Solution (PSI)

PSI bridges the gap by providing a unified and quantifiable score that represents the overall public mood at any given time, serving as a reliable indicator for all stakeholders.

  • A structured database of public perceptions.
  • Statistical and sentiment analysis models generating a single PSI score.
  • Incorporating categories like economy, politics, and business.
  • Providing evidence-based data for better representation.

Aims & Objectives

To develop a scalable and transparent Public Sentiment Index that translates social media emotion data into a dynamic, comparable public perception score.

01

Collect and aggregate social media posts/comments from multiple platforms.

02

Train a Transformer-based model to classify text into granular emotions.

03

Design an ELO-style rating system based on sentiment output.

04

Build a user-friendly dashboard for real-time visualization.
